About Us

Formerly C.B Travel and Tours

Was founded in 2023 as a sole proprietorship. Initially operated as a B2B travel service, C.B Travel and Tours started as a home-based business in Angono. It was focused on providing travel solutions and building corporate partnerships.

City Break Travel OPC

In May 2025, the company transitioned into a corporation and shifted its market focus from B2B to B2C. CBTC expanded services to cater directly to individual travelers and marked a major milestone in growth and business development
